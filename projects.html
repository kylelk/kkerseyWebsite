Mining

I map mining related places in north idaho's silver valley.
one of my favorite places to map is Burke canyon.
Burke canyon is very narrow, it is few hundred feet wide and has many old mines on each side of the canyon.

Mining features i have mapped in the silver valley
  • An old wooden pipeline
  • Ore bins
  • Water flowing from mine audits
  • Mine vents

My favorite mining site i mapped was the Star mine in Burke Idaho.
This is the deepest mine in north america, it is 8100 feet deep.
